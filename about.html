About Waraf

Advancing Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 food security goals through solar-powered vertical farming that leverages the region's abundant sunlight

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2025, Waraf began as a senior design project at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM). Our multidisciplinary team—bringing together expertise from chemical, electrical, mechanical, and industrial engineering—united with a vision to address Saudi Arabia's critical food security challenges as outlined in Vision 2030. In a region facing water scarcity and harsh growing conditions, we recognized the potential to harness the Kingdom's abundant solar resources through innovative vertical farming technology.

What sets Waraf apart is our integrated approach that combines diverse engineering perspectives. We've developed a comprehensive system featuring proprietary algae prevention technology, smart environmental monitoring, and optimized resource management—all powered by Saudi Arabia's plentiful sunlight. This directly contributes to the Kingdom's food security and sustainability objectives.
