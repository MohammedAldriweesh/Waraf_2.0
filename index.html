Why Hydroponics?

95% Less Water

Hydroponic systems recirculate water, reducing usage by up to 95% compared to conventional farming. A typical lettuce crop requires just 1 gallon of water in a hydroponic system versus 20+ gallons in soil-based growing.

90% Less Land

Our vertical stacking technology reduces land requirements by up to 90%. A single module produces the equivalent yield of many times its footprint in traditional farmland, allowing for productive farms in urban environments.

Local Production

Hydroponic systems near consumers reduce transportation carbon emissions by up to 98%. The average fruit or vegetable travels 1,500 miles to market, while locally-grown hydroponics can reduce this to under 50 miles.

Maximum Freshness

Vegetables begin losing nutrients within 24 hours of harvest. Local hydroponics allows for fresh-picked delivery, preserving up to 40% more nutrients than conventional produce that spends days in transit.
