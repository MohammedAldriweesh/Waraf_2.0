Our Products

Innovative hydroponic equipment designed for optimal growing performance

Stacked Vertical Farm Module

Dimensions: 180cm × 60cm × 200cm (L×W×H)
Capacity: 72 Plants (24 per floor) + Nursery
Power: 700W Solar-powered with Battery Backup
Lighting: 40 Energy-efficient LED Grow Lights

Product Description

The Stacked Vertical Farm Module is our flagship hydroponic growing system designed for maximum efficiency in minimal space. Each self-contained unit features three growing floors plus a dedicated nursery area on the bottom level, allowing you to cultivate plants at various growth stages simultaneously.

Completely modular and scalable, these units can function independently or connect with additional modules to create larger growing operations. The system is powered by solar panels with sun-tracking technology and battery storage, making it an environmentally friendly and energy-efficient solution for year-round indoor farming.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive environmental monitoring (EC, TDS, temperature, humidity, CO₂)
  • ESP32-WROOM32 microcontroller for intelligent system management
  • Solar-powered with battery backup
  • Proprietary algae prevention technology
  • Automated nutrient and pH management
  • 5-year historical data access via Waraf platform
